Information on Cancer

About 555,0000 out of 1.237 billion (2012) people died of cancer in India in 2010. There are around 1 million new cancer cases in India every year, and around 14 million in the world every year.

Let No One Fight Alone

It is aptly said “United we stand, Divided we fall” Yoddhas moto is to help patients fight cancer and win this battle. We are covered by the leading print and TV houses and are supporting over 20000 members across the globe.
